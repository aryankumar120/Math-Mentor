SEQUENCES AND SERIES

Arithmetic Progression (AP):
Terms: a, a+d, a+2d, ..., a+(n-1)d
nth term: a_n = a + (n-1)d
Sum of n terms: S_n = n/2 * (2a + (n-1)d) = n/2 * (first + last)
Arithmetic mean of a and b: (a + b) / 2
If a, b, c are in AP: 2b = a + c

Geometric Progression (GP):
Terms: a, ar, ar^2, ..., ar^(n-1)
nth term: a_n = ar^(n-1)
Sum of n terms: S_n = a(r^n - 1)/(r-1) for r != 1; S_n = na for r = 1
Sum of infinite GP (|r| < 1): S = a / (1 - r)
Geometric mean of a and b: sqrt(ab)
If a, b, c are in GP: b^2 = ac

Harmonic Progression (HP):
Reciprocals form an AP: 1/a, 1/(a+d), 1/(a+2d), ...
nth term: 1 / (a + (n-1)d)
Harmonic mean of a and b: 2ab / (a + b)

AM-GM-HM relationship: AM >= GM >= HM (for positive reals)
AM * HM = GM^2

Arithmetic-Geometric Progression (AGP):
Sum of a, (a+d)r, (a+2d)r^2, ... use multiply-and-subtract method:
S = a/(1-r) + dr/(1-r)^2 (for |r| < 1)

Standard summation formulas:
Sum of first n natural numbers: n(n+1)/2
Sum of squares: n(n+1)(2n+1)/6
Sum of cubes: [n(n+1)/2]^2
Sum of first n odd numbers: n^2
Sum of first n even numbers: n(n+1)

Telescoping series: Sum (f(k+1) - f(k)) = f(n+1) - f(1)

Useful tricks:
- If S is series sum, compute rS and subtract to find closed form
- For sum of 1/(k(k+1)): partial fractions 1/k - 1/(k+1), telescopes to 1 - 1/(n+1) = n/(n+1)
- nth term of series found by T_n = S_n - S_{n-1} for n >= 2

Infinite series:
Sum of ar^k from k=0 to inf = a/(1-r) for |r| < 1
Sum of k*r^{k-1} = 1/(1-r)^2 for |r| < 1
